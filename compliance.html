🪪
Age Requirements

Customers must be 21 years of age or older to purchase products through Orbitz. Age verification is required before any order is fulfilled.

🔍
Identification Verification

Valid government-issued photo identification may be required before fulfillment or delivery. Acceptable forms include a state-issued driver's license, passport, or military ID.

📍
Delivery Restrictions

Delivery availability depends on local laws and approved service areas. Orbitz currently serves select jurisdictions in the Washington DC metro area. Availability may change based on regulatory updates.

🧠
Responsible Consumption

Do not drive or operate machinery while impaired. Cannabis affects individuals differently. Start low, go slow, and consume in a safe environment. Keep all products away from children and pets.

⚖️
Possession Limits

Customers are responsible for understanding and complying with applicable local possession limits. Possession laws vary by jurisdiction and it is the customer's responsibility to remain within legal limits.

🚫
Interstate Transportation

Products purchased through Orbitz may not be transported across state lines. Cannabis remains a federally controlled substance. Transporting cannabis across state lines is a federal offense regardless of destination state law.

🩺
Medical Disclaimer

Products and information provided through Orbitz are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. Consult a licensed healthcare professional before use if you have medical concerns.

📋
Regulatory Changes

Cannabis laws vary by jurisdiction and may change over time. Orbitz makes every effort to remain current with applicable regulations. Customers should stay informed about laws in their local area.
